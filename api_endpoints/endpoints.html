Airbnc

        Airbnc is an online marketplace connecting hosts and guests worldwide for short-term rentals.
        Hosts can earn money by renting out space in their homes or entire properties. 
        Also allow guest to give ratings and reviews on properties and can add properties in favourites.
        And finally allow to reserve bookings.

List of endpoints for Airbnc
